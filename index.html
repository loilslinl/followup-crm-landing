FAQ

Everything you're wondering

Do I need to know how to code to use FollowUp?
+
Not at all. FollowUp is built for freelancers, agency owners, and sales reps — not developers. Setting up your first automation takes about 2 minutes with a simple point-and-click rule builder. No code, no tech skills required.
How does the automated follow-up actually work?
+
You create a simple rule — for example: "If a lead hasn't replied in 3 days, send this SMS." FollowUp monitors all your leads in the background and fires the message automatically at the right time. You can set up multiple rules to create full follow-up sequences. You don't have to do anything once the rule is live.
What's the difference between the Free and Pro plans?
+
The Free plan lets you manage up to 25 leads with 1 automation rule — great for getting started. Pro ($29/mo) unlocks 500 leads, 10 automation rules, full SMS and email automation, CSV import, the Kanban pipeline, and AI lead scoring. If you're actively chasing deals, Pro pays for itself with a single closed lead.
Will the messages look like they came from a robot?
+
No. Every message uses your templates with personalised variables like {{name}} and {{company}} swapped in automatically. Your leads receive what looks like a personal message from you. The AI message generator also writes context-aware follow-ups based on your notes — so each message fits the conversation.
What happens when a lead replies?
+
Great question. When a lead replies, FollowUp automatically pauses any pending follow-ups for that lead, marks their status as Replied, boosts their lead score, and notifies you. The last thing you want is an automated follow-up firing after someone's already responded — FollowUp handles that for you.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel from your settings in one click and you won't be charged again. Your data stays accessible until the end of your billing period.
Does it work for agencies managing multiple clients?
+
Yes. Many of our users are agency owners running follow-up sequences for 10–20 active leads at a time across different client pipelines. The Growth plan ($49/mo) gives you unlimited leads and automations — designed exactly for that use case.
